Privacy Policy

Orbit - Mental Health AI

Last Updated: June 29, 2025

Introduction

James Bayless ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Orbit - Mental Health AI mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App and the choices you have associated with that data.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We collect several types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our service to you:

Personal Information:

  • Email address (for account creation)
  • Name (optional, for personalization)
  • Profile information you choose to provide

Health-Related Information:

  • Mood entries and emotional states
  • CBT session responses and thoughts
  • Habit tracking data
  • Mindfulness and meditation progress
  • Journal entries and notes

Usage Data:

  • App features used and frequency
  • Session duration and engagement
  • Device information (iOS version, device type)
  • Crash reports and performance data

AI Conversation Data:

  • Messages sent to the AI therapist
  • AI-generated responses
  • Conversation context and history

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • To personalize your experience and AI responses
  • To track your mental health progress
  • To provide CBT exercises and recommendations
  • To sync data across your devices (if enabled)
  • To send you reminders and notifications (if enabled)
  • To improve our App and develop new features
  • To provide customer support
  • To detect and prevent technical issues

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in multiple locations for security and functionality:

Local Storage:

  • Encrypted on your device using iOS security features
  • Remains on device unless you enable cloud sync
  • Can be used offline without internet connection

Cloud Storage (Optional):

  • Firebase Firestore for cross-device sync
  • Firebase Authentication for account management
  • Encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Stored on Google Cloud Platform servers

Security Measures:

  • Industry-standard encryption protocols
  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Regular security updates
  • Access controls and authentication

Note: While we implement strong security measures, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

AI and Third-Party Services

Google Gemini AI:

  • We use Google's Gemini AI to power therapeutic conversations
  • Your messages are processed by Google's AI services
  • Google does not store your personal health data
  • AI interactions are subject to Google's privacy policies

Firebase Services:

  • Authentication and data storage
  • Analytics (privacy-preserving, no personal data)
  • Crash reporting for app stability

Google AdMob (Free Users):

  • Displays advertisements to free tier users
  • Uses device advertising ID
  • You can opt out of personalized ads in iOS settings
  • Premium subscribers see no advertisements

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in these circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations or court orders
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • In connection with a merger or acquisition (with notice)
  • With service providers who assist in app operations (under strict confidentiality)

We will NEVER share your mental health data with:

  • Insurance companies
  • Employers
  • Marketing companies
  • Data brokers

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

Access and Portability:

  • Export your data at any time (Premium feature)
  • Request a copy of all your personal data

Modification:

  • Update your profile information
  • Edit or delete journal entries
  • Modify mood and health records

Deletion:

  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Remove specific entries or records
  • Clear AI conversation history

Control:

  • Disable cloud sync to keep data local only
  • Turn off notifications
  • Opt out of analytics
  • Control data sharing preferences

Children's Privacy

Our App requires users to be at least 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

For users aged 13-17:

  • We recommend parental involvement
  • Parents can request access to their child's data
  • Parents can request deletion of their child's account

If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will promptly delete it.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Active account data: Retained while account is active
  • Deleted account data: Removed within 30 days
  • Backup data: Removed within 90 days
  • Analytics data: Anonymized after 12 months
  • Crash reports: Retained for 90 days

You can request immediate deletion at any time by contacting us.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of your state, province, country, or governmental jurisdiction.

If you are located outside the United States and choose to use our App, please note that we transfer data to the United States and process it there.

By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification for material changes

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information we collect
  • Right to know if we sell or share personal information (we don't)
  • Right to opt-out of sale (not applicable as we don't sell data)
  • Right to delete your personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.
