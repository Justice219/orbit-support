‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private and secure?
Yes! All your personal data is encrypted and stored locally on your device. We use industry-standard encryption and never share your personal information with third parties.
How does the AI coaching work?
Our AI uses Google's Gemini AI with cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) techniques to help reframe negative thoughts and provide personalized guidance. Your conversations are processed securely through Google's AI services.
Can I sync my data across devices?
Yes, the app requires Firebase for account management and data synchronization. Your data is securely encrypted and synchronized across your devices through Google's Firebase platform.
Is Orbit a replacement for therapy?
No, Orbit is a wellness tool designed to complement professional mental health care. For serious mental health concerns, please consult with a licensed mental health professional.
How much does Orbit cost?
Orbit offers a free tier with core features and a premium subscription for advanced AI coaching, unlimited cloud sync, and additional mindfulness content.
Can I delete my data?
Absolutely! You can delete all your data at any time from the app settings. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.
